📝 Design Philosophy

These characters are designed in a more realistic & professional style compared to typical 8-bit RPG characters. They feature:

  • 32x32 pixel base sprites (scalable to any size)
  • Professional color schemes (blues, grays, earth tones)
  • Distinct personalities through clothing colors
  • Canvas 2D rendering (no external assets)
  • Easy customization (change hair, shirt, pants colors)
  • Multiple outfit variations possible
